Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Overview
This project is an internal automation tool that uploads videos to the YouTube channel VR-Shorts. It is NOT a public service.

What data is accessed

  • Google account identity (via OAuth) solely to grant the app permission to upload videos to the channel.
  • No additional personal data is collected, stored, or shared beyond what is necessary for OAuth and the YouTube API.

How data is stored

OAuth client ID/secret and refresh tokens are stored securely in Google Secret Manager and used by the pipeline running in Cloud Run. They are not publicly accessible.
